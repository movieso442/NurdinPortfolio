I'm Kewangang Muhammed Nurdin, a cybersecurity student and secure software developer based in Bamenda, Cameroon. I'm the founder of CyberNurdin and co-founder of Bauhaven, and I spend most of my time building secure server-side systems and the web and mobile apps that sit on top of them.

My recent work includes a mentorship application workflow with role-based access control, JWT authentication, and an application-review workflow. I care about clean architecture and getting the fundamentals right.
